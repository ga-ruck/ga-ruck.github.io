    ELEBEA WAS CREATED IN SEPTEMBER 2007, FIRS UNDER THE NAME OF LIBRE ALBEDRIO, WICH MEANS FREE WILL IN SPANISH. AT THE BEGINNING IT WAS ALL ABOUT EXPERIMENTING AND HAVING FUN, CREATING ILLUSTRATIONS AND CHARACTERS TO UPLOAD ON FLICKR AND PAINTING WALLS AROUND CORDOBA, IN ARGENTINA.
     
    WITH THE TIME, IT EXPANDED TO WORK WITH CLIENTS OFFERING COMMISSIONED ILLUSTRATIONS, HOSTING WORKSHOPS IN THE STUDIO, PARTICIPATING IN ART EXHIBITIONS AND POP UP SHOPS AROUND TEL AVIV AND OTHER CITIES OF THE WORLD.
     
    IF YOU WANT TO READ MORE ABOUT THE CREATIVE PROCESS YOU CAN CLICK HERE AND READ THE FULL INTERVIEW FOR THREADLESS.
     
     
    FOLLOW ELEBEA ON INSTAGRAM AND FACEBOOK FOR DAILY UPDATES AND FUN STORIES.
